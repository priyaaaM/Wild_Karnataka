Udupi

Located in the Coastal Region of Karnataka

Udupi, a beautiful coastal town in Karnataka, is known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient temples, and serene beaches. Famous for its iconic Udupi Sri Krishna Temple, the town draws devotees and tourists alike. With its scenic beaches, including Malpe and Kaup, and its world-renowned cuisine, Udupi is a perfect blend of spirituality and coastal charm.... Udupi is not just about temples; it is also famous for its cuisine, particularly the Udupi-style vegetarian food that has won hearts globally. The Malpe beach, with its golden sands, and the St. Mary's Island, with its unique hexagonal basalt rock formations, are popular tourist spots.
