Coorg - Scotland of India

Located in Kodagu, Karnataka

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is one of the most scenic hill stations in southern India, located in the Western Ghats of Karnataka. Known for its lush green landscapes, coffee plantations, and beautiful weather, Coorg is often referred to as the "Scotland of India." The district is home to a rich culture, ancient temples, and a variety of wildlife, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.... Coorg is famous for its coffee estates that spread across the rolling hills, offering a picturesque view of the valleys below. The region is also home to several picturesque waterfalls such as Abbey Falls and Iruppu Falls. Adventure seekers can explore the many trekking trails, enjoy river rafting in the Cauvery River, or visit the Dubare Elephant Camp, where one can interact with elephants.
