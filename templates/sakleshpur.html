Shakleshpur

Located in the Western Ghats, Hassan District, Karnataka

Shakleshpur is a serene hill station located in the Hassan district of Karnataka, nestled in the picturesque Western Ghats. Known for its lush green coffee plantations, misty hills, and tranquil atmosphere, Shakleshpur is a perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful getaway. The town offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, rivers, and valleys, making it a hidden gem in southern India.... Shakleshpur is often referred to as the "Land of Coffee" due to its expansive coffee plantations. The town is also home to several beautiful waterfalls, such as the Manjarabad Fort, which provides a panoramic view of the Western Ghats. Visitors can explore the rolling hills, take scenic drives through coffee estates, and enjoy activities like trekking, birdwatching, and camping. The area is rich in biodiversity, with a variety of flora and fauna, making it a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts as well. The cool climate and lush greenery make Shakleshpur an ideal escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
